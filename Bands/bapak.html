Miasma Tahun Asu

Released: 2020
Genres: Experimental Rock
6.5/10

The experimental project of Miasma Tahun Asu mingles with a variety of genres, with no real stylistic flow. Black Heron Intro is a genre bending psychedelic extravaganza. Jon Devoight and Dogma Milenial Provinsi Yggdrasil are two aggressive and experimental rock pieces. Orpheus LIVE From the Underworld is a vulnerable and emotional soft song. While the album may cover a variety of styles, the pacing starts to suffer and the genre switchups start to feel somewhat overdone.

Highlights: Black Heron Intro, Jon Devoight, Dogma Milenial Provinsi Yggdrasil, Orpheous LIVE From the Underworld, An Angel at My Table 1 and 3
