Biography

The Brave Little Abacus were a Midwest Emo band from Sandown, New Hampshire. Formed in 2007 by vocalist and guitarist Adam Demirjian, keyboardist Zach Kelly-Onett, and bassist Andrew Ryan (later adding drummer Nick Morrone). The band would go one to record 2 albums, 2 EPs, and a split with Matt Aspinwall.
