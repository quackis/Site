Biography

Black Country, New Road (commonly abbreviated to BCNR or BC,NR) are an English rock band formed in Cambridgeshire in 2018, consisting of Tyler Hyde (bass), Lewis Evans (saxophone), Georgia Ellery (violin), May Kershaw (keys), Charlie Wayne (drums) and Luke Mark (guitar). The band's first two albums featured guitarist and lead vocalist Isaac Wood, who left the band in 2022.

Discography

For the First Time

Released: 2021
Genres: Post-Rock, Post-Punk
6.5/10

After releasing three singles, including the passionate and aggressive Sunglasses, Black Country, New Road debuted with For the First Time. The album contained the three singles and two new tracks, however they reworked the impressive Sunglasses to a more mellow and subdued version. The intro Instrumental contains horns, tribal drumming, and a repetitive keyboard rhythm. Athens, France is their tribute to Slint, containing very similar guitar and drumming patterns. Science Fair is another Slint-like narrative piece, containing awkward vocals, leading to a noisy and theatric ending. Track X is a slow buildup, starting almost hollow, but doesn't lead to much. Opus closes the album with a speedy and intricate instrumental, in contrast with slow and hushed vocals.

Highlights: Science Fair, Sunglasses, Opus

Ants From Up There

Released: 2022
Genres: Art Rock, Post-Rock
6.5/10

Black Country, New Road changed their pace and style for their second release, embracing an Arcade Fire-like sound. Chaos Space Marine starts the album off with that sound. Concorde continues that along with a four-minute slow buildup. Good Will Hunting leads slowly into an emotional climax. The Place Where He Inserted the Blade is a melodic piece, balancing between slow, instrumental passages and climactic releases. Snow Globes takes a while to buildup, but peaks with speedy drumming and frantically yelled vocals before returning to its slow orchestral state. Basketball Shoes meanders too long without enough interest to reach it's peak. Many of the longer tracks on the album suffer from this problem.

Highlights: Concorde, Good Will Hunting, The Place Where He Inserted the Blade, Snow Globes
